Tips & Tricks

Remember to measure

While it can be very easy to get carried away and want to start cutting and glueing (we've all been there), taking the time to measure out your pieces will help your scrapbook page designs look more professional and put together.

Use the right kind of glue

Depending on the kind of paper and materials you're planning on using in your scrapbook you will want to be sure to use the proper kind of glue. This will ensure that your materials will stay in place for years to come.

Make sure your cutting blade is sharp

When it comes to blades it is very important to use a blade that is sharp. Using an old or dull blade can not only make the cutting process more difficult, but it can be more risky in terms of accidentally injuring yourself. We're just trying to look out for your fingers.
