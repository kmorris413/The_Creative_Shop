Tips & Tricks

Stay organized

There are a lot of really fun supplies to check out when it comes to sewing, however things can get messy and tangled up very easily. Having an organized space for all of your materials and tools can help you move through your projects with ease and enjoyment.

Go slow

When you first start using your sewing machine remember to take it slow. There is no rush and you will have more success if you move slowly when learning how your machine works.

Have a good work space

Dedicating a work space for your sewing projects can also be very helpful. Look for a space that offers plenty of light, space to spread out all your fabric and most importantly a space you can feel comfortable in. P.S. we have a great selection of sewing chairs that will make things even more comfy.
